# ShareBay
The Project entitle “ShareBay” is based on the Share Mobility Business Model of Electric Vehicles. Shared mobility is the short-term access to shared vehicles according to the user’s needs and convenience. However, this business model has been considered as a downside due to increasing pollution across the urban cities.
